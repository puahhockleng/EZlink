Apply for ITE Student Concession Card

 

Follow the steps below to submit a photo to apply:

 

Step 1

  • Prepare a digital colour photograph
  • It must show full face (without headgears); taken against plain white background without shadow; must be taken within the last 3 months

Cropping Tool

Step 2 

  • Click here: https://bit.ly/crop-photo
  • Select the photo from step 1
  • Crop your photo
  • Save the photo

For iPhone, you need to click & hold on the cropped image to save to your photo gallery!

Submission

Step 3 

  • Click here: https://bit.ly/reg-sub
  • Login using your userid [Full NRIC, with the last letter] and your password
  • Upload your cropped & Saved photo
  • Submit

 

 

For students with a valid MOE Smart Card: extend concession validity till 31 Mar 2022
