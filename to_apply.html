To make ITE Student Concession Card by yourself

You did not submit photo online successfully before the cut off for mass printing (Jan/Apr 2020 intake). Transit Link HQ has uploaded your data into their system, ready for you to make your new card.

You just need to make your ITE Concession Card by yourself at any Transit Link Concession Card Replacement Offices (locations are listed below).

Procedure

Go to any Transit Link Concession Card Replacement Offices to print and collect your card on the spot.

You need to bring

  1. your NRIC
  2. a recent coloured passport-size photograph taken against white background
  3. $5 top up amount [cost of your new card and printing fee will be paid by ITE]

Transit Link Concession Card Replacement Offices locations

  • Ang Mo Kio Bus Interchange
  • Choa Chu Kang Bus Interchange
  • Jurong East Bus Interchange
  • Tampines Bus Interchange
  • Hougang Central Bus Interchange
  • Admiralty MRT Station
  • Buona Vista MRT Station
  • Somerset MRT Station**
  • Bukit Panjang MRT Station **

Operating Hours: 10.00AM to 6.00PM Daily, Closed on Public Holidays

**closed for meal break from 2pm to 3pm
