To make ITE Student Concession Card by yourself

You did not submit photo online successfully before the cut off for mass printing. Transit Link HQ has uploaded your data (without photo) into their system, ready for you to make your new card.

You must visit any Transit Link Concession Card Replacement Offices (locations at the bottom) to make your ITE Concession Card by yourself.

Procedure

Go to any Transit Link Concession Card Replacement Offices to print and collect your card immediately.

You need to bring

  1. your NRIC
  2. a recent coloured passport-size photograph taken against white background
  3. $5 top up amount

[cost of your new card and printing fee will be paid by ITE]

  • Choa Chu Kang Bus Interchange (digital photo taking service available at this outlet at fee, $2)
  • Jurong East Bus Interchange (digital photo taking service available at this outlet at fee, $2)
  • Tampines Bus Interchange
  • Hougang Central Bus Interchange
